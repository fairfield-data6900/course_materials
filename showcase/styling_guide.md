# Showcase Styling Guide — AI & Tech Institute

**For:** All teams presenting at the May 7 Showcase
**Purpose:** Consistent, professional look across all projects

---

## Colors

Use these exact values. No other reds, no other grays.

| Role | Name | Hex | Use for |
|:-----|:-----|:----|:--------|
| **Primary** | Fairfield Red | `#E03A3E` | Headings, accent bars, key highlights |
| **Secondary** | Dolan Red | `#C41230` | Logo lockup, buttons, links |
| **Dark** | Charcoal | `#231F20` | Body text, borders |
| **Light** | Warm Gray | `#F5F5F3` | Slide backgrounds, table fills |
| **White** | White | `#FFFFFF` | Slide backgrounds, text on red |

**Rule:** Red is for emphasis, not everything. A slide with too much red looks like a warning sign, not a brand.

---

## Fonts

Use system fonts — no installs required.

| Role | Font | Weight | Size (slides) | Size (PDF) |
|:-----|:-----|:-------|:---------------|:-----------|
| **Headings** | Calibri | Bold | 28–36pt | 16–20pt |
| **Body** | Calibri | Regular | 18–22pt | 11pt |
| **Code/Data** | Consolas or Courier New | Regular | 16–18pt | 10pt |
| **Captions** | Calibri | Light or Italic | 14pt | 9pt |

## Slides

### Layout Rules

- **Title slide:** AI Institute logo (top-left or centered), project name, team names, date, "AI & Tech Institute" tagline
- **Content slides:** AI Institute logo small in bottom-left or bottom-right corner — pick one position, keep it consistent
- **Last slide:** "AI & Tech Institute" + institute URL + team contact info
- **Aspect ratio:** 16:9
- **Max text per slide:** 6 lines or 30 words — if you need more, split into two slides

### Slide Structure for Showcase (5 min)

| Slide | Content | Time |
|:------|:--------|:-----|
| 1 | Title + team | 0:00 |
| 2 | The problem (who feels this pain?) | 0:30 |
| 3 | The solution (what does your skill do?) | 1:30 |
| 4 | How it works (simple architecture — one diagram) | 3:00 |
| 5 | Value proposition (what changes for the user?) | 4:00 |
| 6 | Transition to demo | 4:30 |

### Dos and Don'ts

| Do | Don't |
|:---|:------|
| White background with red accents | Red background with white text (hard to read) |
| One idea per slide | Bullet-point walls |
| Show a diagram or screenshot | Show raw code on slides |
| Use the AI Institute logo as provided | Stretch, recolor, or crop the logo |

## PDF (HANDOFF.md export)

### Formatting

- **Margins:** 1 inch all sides
- **Header:** AI Institute logo (small, top-right) + document title (top-left)
- **Footer:** Page number (centered) + "AI & Tech Institute — Fairfield Dolan" (bottom-left)
- **Headings:** Fairfield Red (`#E03A3E`), Calibri Bold
- **Body:** Charcoal (`#231F20`), Calibri Regular, 11pt
- **Tables:** Warm Gray (`#F5F5F3`) alternating row fill, thin Charcoal borders
- **Links:** Dolan Red (`#C41230`), underlined

## Required Branding Elements

Every deliverable (slides + PDF) must include:

1. **AI Institute logo** — use the provided file, do not recreate
2. **"AI & Tech Institute"** — spelled exactly this way, every time
3. **Institute URL** — `https://www.fairfield.edu/.../ai-and-tech-institute/` on the final slide and in the PDF
4. **Team attribution** — names and roles visible on title slide and PDF cover
